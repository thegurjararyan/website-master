Gurjar Mahotsav 2024 Uttar Pradesh

An attempt to connect with the Gurjars from all over the world.

A call of Clarion to announce the first of its kind, a unique show of ethnicity and culture on 23, 24 & 25 Dec 2024, at Surajkund Mela ground.

Here you will be able to witness the emergence of a culturally vibrant and throbbing Gurjar community that is leaving behind the cold contempt and neglect of thousands of years, coping well to keep pace with the Contemporary Times and rising slowly and steadily at all levels of performance, be it art, education, sports or politics at both national and international levels.

The authentic art and artisanship, craft and culture, dress and dishes, dance and dialect, trade, and traditions of large-bodied, simple-hearted Gurjars will be on display here.

Come one and all; bring family and neighbors.

Watch, participate, and get a chance to be a part of making and creating history.
